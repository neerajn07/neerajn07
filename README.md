## 🛰️ What I do
I work on computational workflows for UAV-based geophysical data, with a focus on radiometric and magnetic surveys. My work combines forward modelling, inversion, and data processing to understand how survey design and acquisition conditions influence the final results.

### 🛠️ Technical Focus
- **Survey design:** Evaluating line spacing, flight height, and sampling density relative to detector footprint and spatial resolution  
- **Data processing:** Flight log synchronization, QC/QA, and noise assessment (including counting statistics)  
- **Modeling & inversion:** 3D forward modelling and inversion for gravity, magnetic, and radiometric data using in-house codes  
- **Simulation:** Development of **RadSIMU**, a survey-scale simulator for UAV gamma-ray spectrometry  
- **Machine learning (applied):** Lithological mapping using radiometric and magnetic data with spatial validation
